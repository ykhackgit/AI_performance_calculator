Charts & Explanation

The calculator computes:
  • Weight storage from params & precision.
  • KV cache per token ≈ 2 × Layers × Hidden × dtype bytes.
  • Compute roof: (NGPUs × TFLOPS × utilization) / (FLOPs per token).
  • Bandwidth roof: MemBandwidth / bytes-per-token.
  • Final throughput = min(compute, bandwidth).
